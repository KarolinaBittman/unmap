## CLUSTER 1 — WHO AM I?
*Self-awareness, personality, identity*

**1. Enneagram of Personality**
Creator: Gurdjieff / Ichazo / Naranjo (modern form)
Core: 9 personality types defined by core fear and core desire. Goes deeper than surface behavior into motivation.
App use: Identity mapping, understanding sabotage patterns
IP note: Concept is free. Specific test questions (Riso-Hudson etc.) are proprietary.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**2. Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)**
Creator: Isabel Briggs Myers & Katharine Cook Briggs (based on Jung)
Core: 16 personality types across 4 dimensions. Most widely used personality framework globally.
App use: Self-understanding, career direction
IP note: The MBTI instrument is proprietary. The 16-type framework and Jungian concepts are free.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**3. Big Five Personality Traits (OCEAN)**
Creator: Various researchers (Costa & McCrae formalized it)
Core: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism. Most scientifically validated model.
App use: Baseline personality assessment
IP note: Freely available and in public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**4. DISC Assessment**
Creator: William Moulton Marston
Core: 4 behavioral styles — Dominance, Influence, Steadiness, Conscientiousness.
App use: Work style and communication patterns
IP note: Concept free, specific assessments are proprietary
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

**5. CliftonStrengths / StrengthsFinder**
Creator: Donald O. Clifton / Gallup
Core: 34 talent themes. Focus on what you're naturally great at.
App use: Identifying Zone of Genius
IP note: Assessment is proprietary (Gallup). Concept of strengths-focus is free.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**6. VIA Character Strengths**
Creator: Martin Seligman & Christopher Peterson
Core: 24 character strengths organized under 6 virtues. Free online assessment available.
App use: Values and strengths identification
IP note: VIA Survey is freely available. Concepts are open.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**7. Human Design**
Creator: Ra Uru Hu (1987)
Core: Synthesis of astrology, I Ching, Kabbalah, and chakras. 5 energy types with decision-making strategies.
App use: Energy management, decision-making style
IP note: Concept freely available
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

**8. Johari Window**
Creator: Joseph Luft & Harry Ingham (1955)
Core: 4-quadrant model of self-knowledge vs. others' perception. Maps blind spots.
App use: Self-awareness onboarding
IP note: Public domain concept
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**9. Shadow Work**
Creator: Carl Gustav Jung
Core: The unconscious "shadow" contains repressed desires, fears and traits. Integration = wholeness.
App use: Identifying hidden blocks and sabotage patterns
IP note: Jungian concepts are public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**10. Attachment Theory**
Creator: John Bowlby & Mary Ainsworth
Core: 4 attachment styles (secure, anxious, avoidant, disorganized) formed in childhood affecting all relationships.
App use: Understanding relationship patterns and safety needs
IP note: Public domain psychological theory
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**11. Love Languages**
Creator: Gary Chapman
Core: 5 ways people give and receive love: words, acts, gifts, time, touch.
App use: Relationship and lifestyle mapping
IP note: "5 Love Languages" is a trademark. The concept is widely used.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

**12. Transactional Analysis (TA)**
Creator: Eric Berne
Core: Parent, Adult, Child ego states. Scripts we inherited and run unconsciously.
App use: Identifying inherited life scripts and limiting beliefs
IP note: Public domain psychological framework
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

## CLUSTER 2 — WHAT HAPPENED TO ME?
*Trauma, healing, nervous system*

**13. Complex PTSD (CPTSD)**
Creator: Judith Herman (1992) / Pete Walker popularized
Core: Chronic childhood trauma creates pervasive effects on identity, emotion regulation, relationships. Different from single-event PTSD.
App use: CORE framework for understanding blocks and self-sabotage
IP note: Clinical concept, freely referenceable. Pete Walker's book is key resource.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**14. Internal Family Systems (IFS)**
Creator: Richard Schwartz
Core: The mind contains multiple "parts" (Managers, Exiles, Firefighters). True Self can lead when parts are healed.
App use: Inner voice recognition, self-sabotage patterns
IP note: IFS Institute holds trademarks on training. Concepts are widely published.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**15. Polyvagal Theory**
Creator: Stephen Porges
Core: The nervous system has 3 states: safe/social, fight-or-flight, shutdown. Safety is prerequisite for growth.
App use: Explaining why people know what to do but can't do it
IP note: Scientific theory, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**16. Somatic Experiencing (SE)**
Creator: Peter Levine
Core: Trauma lives in the body, not just the mind. Releasing stored tension completes the stress response cycle.
App use: Body-awareness prompts and grounding exercises
IP note: SE International holds training trademark. Concepts freely referenceable.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**17. EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing)**
Creator: Francine Shapiro
Core: Bilateral stimulation helps the brain reprocess traumatic memories that are stuck.
App use: Reference and resource linking — not replicable in app without therapist
IP note: Clinical modality, freely referenceable as resource
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐ (as reference only)

**18. Narrative Therapy**
Creator: Michael White & David Epston
Core: You are not your problem. Externalize problems, rewrite dominant life stories.
App use: Life story reframing, identity separation from trauma
IP note: Public domain therapeutic approach
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**19. Rapid Transformational Therapy (RTT)**
Creator: Marisa Peer
Core: Hypnotherapy-based. Goes directly to root cause beliefs formed in childhood and rewrites them.
App use: Reference and resource linking
IP note: RTT is a trademark of Marisa Peer. Concept of root-cause hypnotherapy is broader.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐ (as reference)

**20. Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)**
Creator: Steven Hayes
Core: Psychological flexibility through acceptance, defusion, values clarification, committed action.
App use: Values-based action planning, dealing with stuck states
IP note: Public domain therapeutic model
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**21. Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)**
Creator: Aaron Beck
Core: Thoughts → feelings → behaviors. Identify and restructure unhelpful thought patterns.
App use: Limiting belief identification and reframing
IP note: Freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**22. Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)**
Creator: Marsha Linehan
Core: Mindfulness + distress tolerance + emotion regulation + interpersonal effectiveness.
App use: Daily regulation tools, crisis management features
IP note: Freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**23. Gestalt Therapy**
Creator: Fritz Perls
Core: Present-moment awareness. "Unfinished business" from the past interrupts present living.
App use: Present-moment awareness prompts
IP note: Public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

**24. Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT)**
Creator: Leslie Greenberg
Core: Emotions contain adaptive information. Processing rather than suppressing emotions leads to healing.
App use: Emotional intelligence features
IP note: Freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**25. Positive Disintegration**
Creator: Kazimierz Dąbrowski
Core: Psychological breakdown can be a gateway to higher-level personality development.
App use: Reframing crisis and transition as growth
IP note: Public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**26. Psychodrama**
Creator: Jacob Moreno
Core: Using role-play and dramatic action to explore and resolve psychological conflicts.
App use: Guided visualization exercises
IP note: Public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐

**27. Family Constellation Therapy**
Creator: Bert Hellinger
Core: Intergenerational trauma patterns. Family system dynamics repeat across generations.
App use: Understanding inherited patterns beyond just personal history
IP note: Freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

**28. Transpersonal Psychology**
Creator: Abraham Maslow / Stanislav Grof (formalized)
Core: Psychology that includes spiritual and transcendent dimensions of human experience.
App use: Meaning and purpose layer beyond ego
IP note: Academic field, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

## CLUSTER 3 — WHAT DO I WANT?
*Purpose, meaning, direction*

**29. Ikigai**
Creator: Japanese cultural concept (Mieko Kamiya academic study)
Core: Intersection of what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, what you can be paid for.
App use: Core purpose mapping tool — natural onboarding centerpiece
IP note: Cultural concept, public domain. The Venn diagram version is a Western interpretation.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**30. Logotherapy**
Creator: Viktor Frankl
Core: The primary human drive is meaning, not pleasure or power. Meaning found through work, love, and suffering.
App use: Purpose discovery for people in crisis or transition
IP note: Public domain. Book "Man's Search for Meaning" widely available.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**31. PERMA Model**
Creator: Martin Seligman
Core: Wellbeing = Positive emotions + Engagement + Relationships + Meaning + Achievement.
App use: Life satisfaction assessment and goal framing
IP note: Public domain scientific model
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**32. Self-Determination Theory (SDT)**
Creator: Edward Deci & Richard Ryan
Core: 3 core psychological needs: Autonomy, Competence, Relatedness. Intrinsic motivation emerges when these are met.
App use: Understanding what drives someone — especially freedom-seekers
IP note: Academic theory, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**33. Design Your Life**
Creator: Bill Burnett & Dave Evans (Stanford)
Core: Apply design thinking to life decisions. Prototype multiple life versions before committing.
App use: Life experimentation and B-point mapping
IP note: Book is trademarked. Methodology concepts are freely referenceable.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**34. Finding Your Why**
Creator: Simon Sinek
Core: Start with purpose (WHY), then HOW, then WHAT. People connect to why, not what.
App use: Purpose statement building
IP note: "Start With Why" is a trademark. The concept is widely taught.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**35. Zone of Genius**
Creator: Gay Hendricks (The Big Leap)
Core: 4 zones — Incompetence, Competence, Excellence, Genius. True fulfillment is in the Zone of Genius.
App use: Career and work direction mapping
IP note: "Zone of Genius" concept from The Big Leap. Widely referenced.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**36. North Star Framework**
Creator: Various (widely used in product strategy and life coaching)
Core: A long-term orienting vision that guides all decisions. Not a goal but a direction.
App use: Vision and B-point articulation
IP note: Generic concept, free to use
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**37. Appreciative Inquiry (5-D Framework)**
Creator: David Cooperrider
Core: Discovery, Dream, Design, Destiny, Define. Focus on what's working, not what's broken.
App use: Strengths-first onboarding approach
IP note: Freely referenceable academic framework
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**38. The 5 Regrets of the Dying**
Creator: Bronnie Ware
Core: Research on deathbed regrets. Most common: not living authentically, working too hard, not expressing feelings.
App use: Values clarification — cutting through noise to what actually matters
IP note: Book concept, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**39. Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs**
Creator: Abraham Maslow
Core: 5-level pyramid from survival → safety → belonging → esteem → self-actualization.
App use: Diagnosing which level someone is stuck on
IP note: Public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**40. The Second Mountain**
Creator: David Brooks
Core: First mountain = achievement and success. Second mountain = commitment, contribution and community.
App use: For users who "succeeded" but still feel empty
IP note: Book concept, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

## CLUSTER 4 — WHAT IS A GOOD LIFE?
*Philosophy and wisdom traditions*

**41. Stoicism**
Creator: Zeno of Citium / Marcus Aurelius / Epictetus / Seneca
Core: Focus only on what you control. Virtue is the highest good. Equanimity in face of uncertainty.
App use: Resilience building, managing uncertainty during transition
IP note: Ancient philosophy, public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**42. Existentialism**
Creator: Sartre / Camus / Heidegger / de Beauvoir
Core: Existence precedes essence. You are not born with a purpose — you create it through choices.
App use: Liberation from inherited identities and "should" thinking
IP note: Public domain philosophy
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**43. Absurdism**
Creator: Albert Camus
Core: Life has no inherent meaning — and the response is not despair but joyful rebellion. Sisyphus is happy.
App use: Reframing uncertainty and meaninglessness as freedom
IP note: Public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**44. Eudaimonia (Aristotelian Ethics)**
Creator: Aristotle
Core: Human flourishing through virtue and living in alignment with your best nature. Not happiness but thriving.
App use: Life satisfaction and virtue-based goal setting
IP note: Public domain ancient philosophy
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**45. Epicureanism**
Creator: Epicurus
Core: Simple pleasures, deep friendships, absence of fear and pain = the good life. Anti-hustle before it was cool.
App use: Redefining what "success" and "enough" actually mean
IP note: Public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**46. Taoism / Wu Wei**
Creator: Laozi (Tao Te Ching)
Core: Go with the natural flow of life. Non-striving. The way that can be named is not the eternal way.
App use: For burned-out overachievers who need to release control
IP note: Ancient tradition, public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**47. Buddhism / The Middle Way**
Creator: Siddhartha Gautama
Core: Suffering comes from attachment. The Eightfold Path leads to liberation. Present-moment awareness.
App use: Non-attachment to outcomes, mindfulness layer
IP note: Ancient tradition, public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**48. Nietzsche — Will to Power / Amor Fati**
Creator: Friedrich Nietzsche
Core: Become what you are. Love your fate (amor fati). Create your own values.
App use: Identity rewriting, embracing rather than fighting your story
IP note: Public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**49. Vedanta / Advaita**
Creator: Ancient Indian philosophical tradition / Adi Shankaracharya
Core: Non-duality. The individual self and universal consciousness are one. Suffering comes from separation illusion.
App use: Deeper meaning layer for spiritually inclined users
IP note: Ancient tradition, public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

**50. Zen Buddhism**
Creator: Bodhidharma / Japanese tradition
Core: Direct experience over intellectual understanding. Beginner's mind. Present-moment simplicity.
App use: Daily mindfulness and beginner's mind prompts
IP note: Ancient tradition, public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**51. Wabi-Sabi**
Creator: Japanese aesthetic philosophy
Core: Beauty in imperfection and impermanence. Nothing is finished, nothing is perfect, nothing lasts.
App use: Self-compassion, releasing perfectionism
IP note: Cultural concept, public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**52. Hygge**
Creator: Danish/Norwegian cultural concept
Core: Coziness, togetherness, simplicity, presence. Quality of life through small everyday pleasures.
App use: Lifestyle design — what does "enough" look like day to day?
IP note: Cultural concept, public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

**53. Ubuntu**
Creator: African philosophical tradition
Core: "I am because we are." Individual identity and wellbeing is inseparable from community.
App use: Community and belonging layer in lifestyle design
IP note: Cultural concept, public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

## CLUSTER 5 — HOW DO I CHANGE?
*Behaviour, identity, transformation*

**54. Identity-Based Habits**
Creator: James Clear (Atomic Habits)
Core: Change starts with identity: "I am the type of person who…" Systems over goals.
App use: Habit and behaviour change features
IP note: "Atomic Habits" is trademarked. Identity-based change concept is widely published.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**55. Transtheoretical Model (Stages of Change)**
Creator: Prochaska & DiClemente
Core: Precontemplation → Contemplation → Preparation → Action → Maintenance. Maps where someone actually is.
App use: Onboarding diagnostic — don't push someone who isn't ready
IP note: Academic model, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**56. Growth Mindset**
Creator: Carol Dweck
Core: Fixed vs growth mindset. Abilities can be developed. Failure is feedback not verdict.
App use: Foundational mindset shift before any other change
IP note: "Mindset" book is trademarked. Growth mindset concept widely published.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**57. Flow State Theory**
Creator: Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi
Core: Optimal experience at the edge of challenge and skill. Time disappears, self disappears, pure engagement.
App use: Identifying activities that create flow = clues to purpose
IP note: Academic theory, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**58. The Hero's Journey**
Creator: Joseph Campbell
Core: Universal narrative: ordinary world → call to adventure → trials → transformation → return.
App use: Framing life change as myth not failure. The journey structure of the app itself.
IP note: Public domain mythological framework
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**59. Liminal Space Theory**
Creator: Arnold van Gennep / Victor Turner (anthropology)
Core: The in-between state during transition. Neither here nor there. Disorienting but creative.
App use: Normalizing the confusion of transition as part of the process
IP note: Academic concept, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**60. Positive Psychology**
Creator: Martin Seligman
Core: Study of what makes life worth living, not just what's wrong. Strengths, flourishing, meaning.
App use: Overarching positive framework vs deficit-based thinking
IP note: Academic field, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**61. Self-Reparenting**
Creator: John Bradshaw / various
Core: As adults we can give our inner child the nurturing we didn't receive. Healing through self-compassion.
App use: CPTSD layer — daily self-reparenting practices
IP note: Therapeutic concept, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**62. Neuroplasticity**
Creator: Donald Hebb / modern neuroscience
Core: The brain rewires through new experiences and repeated thoughts. Change is biologically possible at any age.
App use: Scientific grounding for "it's not too late" messaging
IP note: Scientific concept, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**63. Habit Loop**
Creator: Charles Duhigg (The Power of Habit)
Core: Cue → Routine → Reward. Understanding this loop enables behaviour change.
App use: Daily practice and habit design features
IP note: Concept from "The Power of Habit." Widely published.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**64. Cognitive Defusion (ACT)**
Creator: Steven Hayes
Core: Unhooking from thoughts — observing them rather than being ruled by them. "I notice I'm having the thought that..."
App use: Daily limiting belief work
IP note: Part of ACT framework, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**65. Adlerian Psychology**
Creator: Alfred Adler
Core: Humans are motivated by belonging and significance. Inferiority feelings drive much behavior.
App use: Understanding the roots of comparison and unworthiness
IP note: Public domain
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

## CLUSTER 6 — WHERE AND HOW DO I WANT TO LIVE?
*Lifestyle design, freedom, relocation*

**66. The 4-Hour Workweek**
Creator: Tim Ferriss
Core: Geographic arbitrage, lifestyle design, mini-retirements, outsourcing, automation.
App use: Practical lifestyle freedom framework — the "how" of nomad life
IP note: Book is trademarked. Concepts of lifestyle design widely published.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**67. Cashflow Quadrant**
Creator: Robert Kiyosaki (Rich Dad Poor Dad)
Core: Employee → Self-employed → Business owner → Investor. The rat race is the E and S quadrant.
App use: Financial freedom pathway mapping
IP note: "Rich Dad" is trademarked. Cashflow quadrant concept widely published.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**68. FIRE Movement (Financial Independence, Retire Early)**
Creator: Various (Mr. Money Mustache popularized)
Core: Aggressive saving + investment to reach financial independence and exit traditional employment.
App use: Financial runway calculator and freedom timeline
IP note: Community concept, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**69. Blue Zones**
Creator: Dan Buettner (National Geographic research)
Core: 5 places where people live longest. Common factors: purpose, movement, plants, community, downshift.
App use: Location matching — what environments support long happy lives?
IP note: "Blue Zones" is a trademark. Research findings freely referenceable.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**70. Essentialism**
Creator: Greg McKeown
Core: Less but better. Ruthless elimination of the non-essential. Only do what only you can do.
App use: Simplifying life design — cutting what doesn't serve the B
IP note: Book trademarked. Concept widely published.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**71. Slow Living Movement**
Creator: Cultural movement (Carl Honoré)
Core: Counter to hustle culture. Depth over speed, quality over quantity, presence over productivity.
App use: Alternative lifestyle vision for burned-out users
IP note: Cultural concept, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**72. Third Place Theory**
Creator: Ray Oldenburg
Core: Beyond home (1st) and work (2nd), humans need a 3rd place — café, park, community space — for belonging.
App use: Location matching — what makes a city liveable for a nomad?
IP note: Academic concept, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**73. Nomad Capitalism / Flag Theory**
Creator: Andrew Henderson
Core: Distribute your life legally across multiple countries for freedom, tax efficiency, optionality.
App use: Advanced relocation layer for experienced nomads
IP note: "Nomad Capitalism" is a brand. Geographic diversification concept is free.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐

**74. Ecological Identity**
Creator: Various environmental psychologists
Core: Physical environment shapes identity and capacity. Place is part of self.
App use: Why relocation is not just practical but psychological
IP note: Academic concept, freely referenceable
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**75. Wheel of Life**
Creator: Paul J. Meyer (1960s)
Core: Visual audit of 8-10 life areas scored 1-10. Instantly reveals imbalance.
App use: Core onboarding diagnostic — the perfect starting snapshot
IP note: Concept widely used, generic. Original attributed to Meyer.
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**76. Four Burners Theory**
Creator: David Sedaris / popularized by James Clear
Core: 4 burners: work, family, friends, health. You can only have 2 fully on at a time.
App use: Trade-off awareness in life design
IP note: Widely published concept
Relevance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
